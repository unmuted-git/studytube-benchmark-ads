Benchmark Ads 2026 — 6 Concepts (German copy)
Studytube × Unmuted · LinkedIn Single Image, 1080×1080 · One goal: get people to take the 2-minute benchmark test
For the designer: Each card shows the LinkedIn preview with the intended visual (1080×1080), rebuilt in HTML/CSS as a layout reference — not a finished asset, but the image idea, hierarchy and data points. Below each one is the click-to-copy ad copy plus a short designer note. The ad copy itself stays in German (Sie-form); everything labelled as a note or instruction is in English.

One CTA, everywhere: every ad drives a single action — start the benchmark test. There are two CTAs per ad: the LinkedIn button (Benchmark starten) and a bright CTA pill inside the visual itself (bottom-right). Both should link to the benchmark test (benchmark.studytube.de). Keep the pill prominent — it is the thing we want clicked.
