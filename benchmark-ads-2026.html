Studytube × Unmuted · LinkedIn Single Image, 1080×1080 · Source: L&D Monitor 2026 Benchmark (1,815 DE responses)
For the designer: Each card shows the LinkedIn preview with the intended visual (1080×1080), rebuilt in HTML/CSS as a layout reference — not a finished asset, but the image idea, hierarchy and data points. Below each one is the click-to-copy ad copy plus a short designer note. The ad copy itself stays in German (Sie-form); everything labelled as a note or instruction is in English.

Build logic: The visual carries the provocative number. The intro text raises the bigger problem without repeating that number. The headline closes with a verb. Brand colours: navy #10173f + gradient #3b46ff → #7a3bff. No em dashes, Sie-form, native German tone in all copy.
